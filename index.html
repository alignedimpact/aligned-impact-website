Common Questions
Quick answers.
The questions we hear most before a business books a call. See all questions →
Will my callers know they are not talking to a real person?
AVA is designed to sound professional and natural. Most callers will not know the difference. What they consistently experience is a responsive service that takes their information and gets things moving — which is what matters most.
Do I have to change my existing phone number?
No. You keep your existing number. We provision a new number that AVA answers and you forward your main line to it. Your clients continue calling the number they already know.
How long does setup take?
Most businesses are fully live within 48 hours of completing onboarding. Your AVA script, intake questions, automations, team accounts, and scheduling link — all configured by our team. You review, approve, and go live.
Are there any costs beyond the monthly fee?
Two small ones. A VAPI account for voice and a Twilio account for SMS and your phone number. Both are set up during onboarding and paid directly to those providers. Typical combined cost is $40 to $80 a month depending on call volume. We do not mark them up.
Is there a contract?
No. Month to month from day one. We believe the service should earn your business every month — not hold it hostage.
What if something stops working?
Every dashboard has a Report an Issue button that goes directly to our team. Early partners have direct access and we respond to high-urgency reports within a few hours.
