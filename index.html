How It Works
Up and running in days, not months.
Three simple steps from setup to live service.
1
Your phone gets answered
Every call is answered professionally — day or night, weekends, holidays. Callers are greeted, qualified, and guided through your intake process.
2
Leads are captured and booked
Every caller's information is captured and added to your pipeline automatically. Appointments are booked directly to your team's calendar without you lifting a finger.
3
You track everything in one dashboard
Calls, leads, bookings, no-shows, follow-ups, and Google review requests — all visible in a single clean dashboard for your manager and team.
