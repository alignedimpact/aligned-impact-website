1. Who We Are

This website is operated by Aligned Impact Corp. Aligned Impact Corp ("Aligned Impact," "we," "us," or "our") is the developer and operator of the Business 24/7 platform — an AI-powered call answering, lead capture, appointment booking, SMS automation, and business dashboard service. Our website is located at tryalignedimpact.com and our platform is accessible at alignedimpact.app.

Business 24/7 enables client businesses to deploy AVA, our AI-powered call assistant, to answer inbound calls and send automated SMS communications on their behalf. Aligned Impact Corp acts as a technology service provider and data processor for client businesses. Each client business is independently responsible for their own privacy practices and compliance with applicable law as it relates to their customers.

2. Client Business Privacy & Compliance Requirements

As a condition of using the Business 24/7 platform, all client businesses must agree to and maintain the following privacy and compliance requirements before AVA is activated for their account:

  • Privacy Policy with SMS disclosure: Client businesses must publish a Privacy Policy on their own website that includes an SMS / text messaging section disclosing how customer mobile information is collected, used, and protected, including the required A2P 10DLC carrier disclosure language.
  • Terms of Service with Messaging Terms: Client businesses must include a Messaging Terms section in their Terms of Service covering opt-in and opt-out procedures (STOP/HELP), message frequency, and the "message and data rates may apply" disclosure.
  • AI communications disclosure: Client businesses must disclose to their customers that inbound calls may be answered by an AI assistant and that automated SMS messages may be sent following a call or inquiry. This disclosure must appear on the client's website and, where required by applicable state law, be provided verbally to callers at the start of a call.
  • SMS opt-in language on contact forms: Any contact, booking, or inquiry form on the client's website that collects a mobile phone number must include an unchecked SMS consent checkbox with compliant disclosure language linking to the client's Privacy Policy and Messaging Terms.
  • Call recording compliance: Client businesses operating in two-party consent states are solely responsible for providing any legally required call recording disclosures to their callers.

Aligned Impact Corp provides a Client Compliance Kit during onboarding to help businesses meet these requirements. Clients must confirm that all required language is live on their website before AVA is activated for outbound SMS. Aligned Impact Corp reserves the right to suspend SMS services for any account that does not maintain these standards.

3. Information We Collect

We collect information in two primary contexts: through our own website and platform, and through AVA on behalf of client businesses.

  • Website visitors and prospective clients: When you visit tryalignedimpact.com or submit a contact form, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and any business information you provide.
  • Client business accounts: When a business subscribes to Business 24/7, we collect business name, contact name, email address, billing information, and platform configuration details including AVA script settings, intake questions, and scheduling preferences.
  • Call data collected on behalf of clients: When a caller contacts a client business whose calls are answered by AVA, we collect on that business's behalf: the caller's name, phone number, email address (when provided), reason for calling, appointment preferences, and any other information provided during the call. This data is stored in the client's dashboard and is owned by the client business.
  • SMS communications data: We collect and process mobile phone numbers, opt-in consent records, and message delivery status for SMS communications sent on behalf of client businesses. Opt-in consent records are retained for compliance purposes.
  • Automatically collected data: We collect standard analytics data including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time on site when you visit tryalignedimpact.com.

4. How Information Is Used

Aligned Impact Corp's use of information

Aligned Impact Corp does not use the personal information of client business customers for any purpose of our own. Customer data collected through AVA — including caller names, phone numbers, appointment details, and SMS communications — belongs to the client business. Aligned Impact acts solely as a technology platform: we store that data securely, route it to the appropriate client dashboard, and facilitate the automated communications the client has configured. We do not analyze, profile, market to, or monetize end-user data in any way.

For our own operations, Aligned Impact Corp uses only the information provided directly to us — such as contact form submissions and client account details — to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Business 24/7 platform
  • Respond to inquiries and onboard new client businesses
  • Process payments and manage subscription accounts
  • Maintain records required for legal and regulatory compliance
  • Analyze platform-level usage patterns to improve service quality

Regarding calls answered by AVA

Aligned Impact Corp does not routinely listen to, review, or document calls answered by AVA. Calls are not recorded or retained by Aligned Impact Corp. In the limited circumstance where a client business reports a technical issue and requests our support, an Aligned Impact team member may review relevant call activity solely for the purpose of diagnosing and resolving the issue. Any such review is conducted only at the client's request, is not documented beyond what is necessary to make the needed adjustment, and is not used for any other purpose.

How client businesses are expected to use customer information

Client businesses that use Business 24/7 collect customer information through AVA for the purpose of delivering their own services. As a condition of using our platform, client businesses agree to use customer information only in the following appropriate ways:

  • Confirming, scheduling, rescheduling, or canceling service appointments
  • Sending automated follow-up communications directly related to a specific inquiry or appointment
  • Requesting customer reviews following a completed service interaction
  • Managing their internal lead pipeline and client records
  • Communicating with customers about the specific service the customer contacted them about
  • Sending email communications to customers who have provided their email address, for the same service-related purposes listed above — appointment confirmations, reminders, follow-ups, and review requests
  • Sending marketing SMS messages to customers who have explicitly and separately opted in to receive marketing communications — subject to the consent requirements described below

Transactional vs. marketing SMS — consent requirements for client businesses

The Business 24/7 platform supports two types of outbound SMS communications, and TCPA and carrier rules treat them differently. Client businesses are responsible for understanding and honoring this distinction:

  • Transactional messages (appointment confirmations, reminders, rescheduling notices, cancellation confirmations, and review requests) may be sent to customers who have provided their phone number and consented to receive service-related communications. This consent can be captured through the standard opt-in checkbox on a contact or booking form.
  • Marketing messages (promotional offers, announcements, campaigns, or any message intended to drive a sale or engagement beyond the customer's original inquiry) require a separate, explicit opt-in. A customer who consented to appointment reminders has not consented to marketing messages. Client businesses must obtain and document marketing consent separately before sending any marketing SMS through the platform.

Aligned Impact Corp provides opt-in language templates for both consent types in the Client Compliance Kit. Using marketing SMS without proper separate consent is a TCPA violation and is grounds for immediate suspension of SMS access on the client's account.

Prohibited uses — what client businesses may not do

Client businesses are expressly prohibited from using customer information collected through the Business 24/7 platform in the following ways:

  • Selling, renting, licensing, or transferring customer contact information to any third party for any purpose
  • Sending marketing SMS messages to customers who have not separately and explicitly opted in to receive marketing communications
  • Sending any SMS message — transactional or marketing — to a customer who has replied STOP or otherwise opted out
  • Sharing customer information with affiliates, partners, or other businesses for their independent marketing use
  • Using customer data in a way that is inconsistent with the consent the customer provided at the time of contact
  • Retaining or using customer information after a client's subscription to Business 24/7 has ended, beyond what is required for their own legal compliance obligations

Violation of these prohibited uses is grounds for immediate suspension or termination of a client's Business 24/7 account.

5. How We Share Information

Aligned Impact Corp does not sell personal information — and neither may our clients.

Aligned Impact Corp will never sell, rent, or trade the personal information of end users or client business customers to any third party for any purpose. This is a core commitment of our platform. As a condition of using Business 24/7, all client businesses agree to this same standard: they may not sell or transfer customer information collected through our platform to any third party for marketing, resale, or any independent use.

Beyond the no-sell commitment, we share information only in the following limited and necessary circumstances:

  • With the client business whose number was called: Caller information collected by AVA during an inbound call is delivered to that client business's dashboard. This is the core function of the service — the data belongs to the client and is provided exclusively to them.
  • With infrastructure providers that power the platform: We work with Supabase (secure database), Twilio (SMS and telephony), VAPI (AI voice), Resend (email delivery), and Stripe (payment processing) to operate Business 24/7. Each of these providers receives only the data necessary to perform their specific function and is contractually prohibited from using that data for any other purpose.
  • For legal compliance: We may disclose information when required by law, valid legal process, or court order, or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, the rights of a client, or the safety of others.

6. SMS / Text Messaging & Mobile Data

Aligned Impact Corp may send SMS and MMS messages to individuals who have provided their mobile phone number and affirmatively opted in to receive text communications. These messages may include appointment confirmations, scheduling reminders, follow-up communications, and other service-related notifications delivered through AVA, our AI-powered assistant, on behalf of the business you contacted.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All other categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

How We Use Your Mobile Number

Your mobile phone number is used solely to deliver the service communications you have requested or consented to receive. These include appointment booking confirmations, scheduling changes, reminders, and follow-up messages initiated by AVA on behalf of the business you contacted. Your number will not be sold, rented, or transferred to any third party for their independent use.

Opt-Out

You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive. After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message and no further texts will be sent to your number unless you re-opt in by replying START.

Help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us directly at hello@tryalignedimpact.com.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Contact your mobile carrier for details. Aligned Impact Corp does not charge separately for SMS communications.

Mobile Data Retention

Mobile opt-in records and consent data are retained in accordance with applicable law and Twilio's carrier requirements. This data is used solely for compliance documentation and is not shared with or sold to any third party.

7. AI-Answered Calls

AVA is an AI-powered virtual assistant that answers inbound calls on behalf of service businesses that use our platform. When you call a business that uses Business 24/7, your call is answered by AVA. The following applies:

  • Call recording: Calls may be recorded for quality assurance, training, and service delivery purposes. The business whose number you called is responsible for disclosing call recording to callers as required by applicable state law.
  • Information collected during calls: AVA may collect your name, phone number, the nature of your inquiry, and other information relevant to the service you are requesting. This information is provided directly to the business you called.
  • AI interaction: You are interacting with an AI assistant. AVA is designed to be transparent about being an AI when directly asked.
  • No outbound calls: AVA does not initiate outbound calls. AVA only answers inbound calls placed to a client business's number.
